This page walks you through your first bet on Cyphers, end to end. No code - just the steps you take in the app. You'll be done in a few minutes.

## What you need

- A **Solana wallet**. Phantom, Solflare, Backpack, or any wallet that supports Solana works. If you don't have one, install Phantom from [phantom.app](https://phantom.app).
- A small amount of **SOL** for transaction fees (about `$0.50` is plenty).
- Some **USDC** on Solana. The minimum bet is `$1`, but bring whatever you're comfortable putting up. You can swap SOL for USDC on any Solana DEX (Jupiter, Raydium, Phantom's built-in swap).

<Note>
  Cyphers runs on Solana devnet during the beta. Devnet uses a test USDC mint that has no real value - there's a built-in faucet in the app to top you up.
</Note>

## Place your first bet

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the app">
    Go to the Cyphers web app and click **Connect Wallet** in the top right. Your wallet extension will pop up and ask you to approve the connection.

    <Tip>
      If nothing happens, check that your wallet is unlocked and that it's set to the right network (Devnet during the beta, Mainnet after launch).
    </Tip>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Pick a market">
    Browse the **Markets** page. Each card shows the question, the current odds (how the pool is split between the sides), how much has been bet in total, and how long until the market closes.

    Look for a market in the **betting** phase - that's the only phase that accepts new bets.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Choose a side and an amount">
    Click into a market. You'll see a bet panel with the available sides:

    - For a **YesNo** market: a YES button and a NO button.
    - For a **MultiOutcome** market: one button per option (`2` to `4` options).

    Pick a side. Enter the amount in USDC - the minimum is `$1`. The app shows you the fees before you confirm.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Confirm the transaction">
    Click **Bet**. Your wallet pops up with a transaction to sign. Approve it.

    Behind the scenes:

    - Your browser encrypts your side using a fresh keypair.
    - The transaction submits the encrypted bet to Solana.
    - The Arcium network processes it within around `10` seconds.

    The app shows progress for each step. You don't need to do anything during the wait.
  </Step>

  <Step title="See your position">
    Go to the **Positions** tab. Your bet appears there with:

    - The side you picked.
    - Your stake.
    - The odds you locked in at bet time.
    - The market's status.

    Your side is only visible to you (the app decrypts it locally). Anyone else looking at the chain sees encrypted bytes.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Wait for resolution

Once the market closes:

- Betting stops. No new positions can be opened.
- The resolver posts the real-world outcome.
- The Arcium network decrypts every position and computes payouts (about `10` seconds).
- A 24–48 hour challenge window opens. During this window anyone can flag a wrong-looking resolution.
- After the window passes undisputed, the market becomes claimable.

For the full timeline, see [Market lifecycle](/how-it-works/lifecycle).

## Claim your payout

If you picked the winning side, your position is now claimable.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open your position">
    Go to **Positions** and find the resolved market. The card shows your stake, your side, and your projected payout.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Claim">
    Sign the transaction in your wallet. USDC lands in your account a few seconds later.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  If the market resolved against you, there's nothing to claim. The position stays in your history as a record.
</Note>

If something looks wrong with the resolution, you can dispute it during the challenge window. See [Dispute resolution](/guides/dispute-resolution).
